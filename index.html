Our Story

A Passion for Pastry

"We believe that great baking is an act of love — every croissant folded by hand, every dessert a small masterpiece."

Sweet&Salt Cafe Bakery brings the warmth of European baking tradition to Lincoln Park. Our team crafts each pastry, dessert, and coffee drink with meticulous attention to quality and flavor. From our buttery croissants to our signature shakshuka, every dish is designed to make your day a little sweeter — and a little saltier.

Located on Clark Street in the heart of the neighborhood, we're your local gathering spot for a perfect morning coffee, a leisurely brunch, or an afternoon treat.
